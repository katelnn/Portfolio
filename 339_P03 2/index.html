Nice to meet you!

Hello! My name is Kate Luong and I’m a passionate UI designer based in Vancouver, Canada. I’m interested in the intersection of art and technology and how I can collaborate on projects that challenge conventions but still provides a user-centric experience.

My forte lies in my ability to collaborate and communicate with clients to generate creative concepts that resonate with their target audience. Leveraging a blend of technical skills, I’m able to communicate my thought process and designs to the audience. For designing and prototyping, I’m skilled in using Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe InDesign. On the technical front, my expertise extends to coding languages including Processing, Java, HTML, CSS, and Python.
